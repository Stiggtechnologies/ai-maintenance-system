# SyncAI Enterprise Readiness Suite

Status: working draft for executive, technical, security, procurement, and Asia market-entry conversations.

## Purpose

This folder is the controlled source of truth for claims made to prospective customers, partners, auditors, and investors. No external claim should exceed the evidence recorded here or in the linked repository artifacts.

## Current verified position

The product has a strong, live technical foundation: organization-scoped RLS, database-enforced role controls, TOTP MFA, append-only security logging, human approval of operational recommendations, CI gates, CodeQL, secret scanning, dependency updates, reproducible migrations, a live operating loop, role-specific command centres, RAG-cited reliability agents, ISO 55000 KPI governance, and a 90-day pilot scorecard.

The remaining enterprise blockers are primarily external, operational, or evidence-based rather than new core product capability:

1. A production connector validated against a real historian, CMMS, or ERP environment.
2. Production-domain and hardened-environment alignment.
3. Customer-tenant SSO enforcement and validation.
4. Independent penetration testing.
5. Completion of organizational evidence, formal risk assessment, policy approval, access-review records, vendor due diligence, training records, and audit observation periods.
6. Independent SOC 2 examination and ISO/IEC 27001 certification audit.

## Certification language

Approved language before the independent audits are completed:

> SyncAI has implemented a substantial set of technical controls aligned with SOC 2 and ISO/IEC 27001, including access control, tenant isolation, MFA, audit logging, secure change management, automated security testing, and human-governed AI workflows. Formal certification and independent assurance remain subject to completion of the audit process, organizational evidence review, and the applicable observation period.

Do not state that SyncAI is SOC 2 compliant, SOC 2 certified, ISO 27001 certified, or audit-ready without qualification until the relevant independent body has issued the report or certificate.
