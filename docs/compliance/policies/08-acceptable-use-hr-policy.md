# Acceptable Use & Human Resources Security Policy

**Owner:** `[SECURITY OWNER / PEOPLE OPS]` · **Approved:** `[NAME, DATE]` · **Review:** annual

## 1. Acceptable use

- Use `[COMPANY]` systems and data only for authorized business purposes.
- Protect credentials; use the company password manager; **never share
  credentials in chat, email, or code**; enable MFA.
- Report suspected security events immediately.
- No installation of unauthorized software on systems with data access; keep
  endpoints patched and disk-encrypted.

## 2. Personnel security (joiner / mover / leaver)

- **Screening:** background checks for staff with production/data access, per
  `[jurisdiction and role]`.
- **On hire:** sign NDA + acceptable-use acknowledgement; provision least-
  privilege access via the Access Control Policy.
- **Role change:** re-baseline access.
- **Termination:** revoke all access within `[N hours]`; recover assets;
  offboarding checklist completed and recorded.

## 3. Training & awareness

All staff complete **security-awareness training on hire and annually**;
completion is recorded.

## 4. Disciplinary process

Violations handled per `[HR process]`, up to termination and legal action.

## 5. Evidence (for auditors)

- Signed NDAs/acknowledgements; training-completion records; onboarding/
  offboarding checklists; background-check attestations.
