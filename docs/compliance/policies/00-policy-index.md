# SyncAI Information Security Policy Set — Index

_Starter templates covering the core policies SOC 2 and ISO 27001 expect. Each is
a real, adoptable draft — replace every `[BRACKETED]` field, have the security
owner and leadership sign off (record the date + approver), communicate to all
staff, and review at least annually._

**How auditors use these:** they check (a) the policy exists and is approved,
(b) it reflects what you actually do, and (c) you have evidence you follow it.
A policy that contradicts practice is worse than none — keep them true.
