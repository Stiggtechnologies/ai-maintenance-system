### Upgrading Your Plan

1. **Settings â†’ Billing â†’ "Upgrade Plan"**
2. **Select New Plan**
3. **Review Changes:**
   - New features unlocked
   - Price difference
   - Prorated charges (if mid-cycle)
4. **Confirm Upgrade**

**Prorated Billing:**
- Upgrade mid-month? Pay only for remaining days
- Example: Upgrade on day 15 of 30 â†’ 50% prorated charge

### Downgrading Your Plan

1. **Settings â†’ Billing â†’ "Change Plan"**
2. **Select Lower Tier Plan**
3. **Review Limitations:**
   - Features you'll lose
   - User seat reductions
   - Support changes
4. **Confirm Downgrade**

**Effective Date:**
- Downgrades take effect at end of current billing cycle
- No immediate access changes
- Unused time credited to next bill

### Cancelling Your Subscription

**Before you cancel**, consider:
- **Pause Instead:** Take a break (up to 3 months), pay nothing
- **Downgrade:** Move to Starter plan ($299/mo)
- **Archive Mode:** $99/mo to preserve data without active features

**To Cancel:**

1. **Settings â†’ Billing â†’ "Cancel Subscription"**
2. **Tell us why** (optional feedback)
3. **Confirm Cancellation**

**What Happens:**
- Access continues until end of billing period
- No future charges
- Data retained for 90 days
- Can reactivate within 90 days without data loss

### Reactivating

**Within 90 days:**
1. Log back in
2. Click "Reactivate Subscription"
3. Choose plan
4. Enter payment info
5. All data restored instantly

**After 90 days:**
- Data permanently deleted
- Start fresh (no data recovery)

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Is the free trial really free?**  
A: Yes! No credit card required. Full access for 14 days.

**Q: Can I change plans anytime?**  
A: Yes. Upgrade immediately, downgrades effective next billing cycle.

**Q: What happens to my data if I cancel?**  
A: Retained for 90 days. Reactivate anytime to restore. After 90 days, permanently deleted.

**Q: Do you offer refunds?**  
A: Yes, prorated refunds for annual plans cancelled early. Monthly plans: no refunds, but access continues until cycle end.

**Q: Can I get an invoice before payment?**  
A: Yes (Enterprise only). Set up invoicing + Net 30 payment terms.

**Q: Are there setup fees?**  
A: No setup fees on any plan.

**Q: Can I add users without upgrading?**  
A: Yes, pay per additional user ($50/user/mo on Starter/Pro).

**Q: Is there a long-term contract?**  
A: No. Month-to-month or annual (your choice). Cancel anytime.
